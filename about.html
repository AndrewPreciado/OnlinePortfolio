About Me
Andrew began his journey the moment he was able to pick up a pencil. Every child is exposed to cartoons and he was no different, but after each episode he always asked "how?" How can one create something and give it life? After years of drawing his own Bugs Bunny comics, he wanted to expand his knowledge and pursued a Graphic Design degree from Monmouth University. At the end of his college career he was exposed to animation. With a Undergrad degree under his belt, Andrew began taking all he learned and created his own character models, stories, and self taught himself basic animation principals. It wasn't long until he created his own animated series for the popular YouTube channel "Secular Talk", as well as other animated parody projects for the web in his spare time. To this day, he continues to hone his skills to better his craft and be the best designer and animator he can possibly be. "It wasn't a waste of time if you learned something." This is a quote he lives by every single day. Outside of designing and animating, Andrew enjoys playing Monopoly with anyone that has three hours to spare.
